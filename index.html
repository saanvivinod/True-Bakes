About True Bakes

Since 2018, True Bakes has been bringing joy to Mysuru with handcrafted cakes, cupcakes, and artisan bakes made fresh daily.

Every creation is baked with love, using quality ingredients and traditional techniques. From classic favorites to custom celebration cakes, we pour our passion into every batch.

We believe in small-batch baking to ensure freshness and quality. Each order is made with care, tailored to your preferences, and ready to make your moments special.
